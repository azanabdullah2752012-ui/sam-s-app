How to Play 📖

Your guide to becoming an Eco-DIY Master!

1. Pick Your Stuff 📦

Look around your house for clean, safe recyclable materials. This could be Cardboard Boxes from a delivery, empty Plastic Bottles, Old Clothes you don't wear anymore, Glass Jars, or Tin Cans.

Select what material you have in the first dropdown box on the main page.

2. Choose Your Goal 🎯

What do you want to build today? Choose from:

  • Stuff Holder: Make organizers, caddies, and storage for your room.
  • Pretty Decoration: Create art, lanterns, and cool display pieces.
  • Garden Fun: Build planters, seed starters, and garden helpers.
  • Cool Accessories: Make shields, rings, bags, and fun wear.

Select your goal in the second dropdown box.

3. Get Ideas & Create! 💡

Click the Get Project Ideas! 💡 button to see a list of curated projects that match your materials and goals.

Want to invent your own? Click Create Custom! ✍️ to add your very own project, complete with a title, description, and steps. Your creations will appear at the top of the search results with a special YOUR CREATION badge!

4. Use the Project Planner 📝

Click on any project card to open the Project Planner. Here you will find a checklist of Stuff You Need and Step-by-Step instructions.

Check off each item as you find it and each step as you complete it. When you finish every checkbox, you'll get a magical confetti celebration! 🎉

5. Review My Work! ⭐

Finished building? We want to see it!

  • Click 📸 Snap Photo to upload a picture of your completed project.
  • Click Review My Work! ⭐.

Our Magic Brain AI will look at your picture and give you an expert review, a tip, and a star rating from 1-10!
