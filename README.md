# 🎨 Eco Remix Studio

**Turning today's recycling into tomorrow's treasures!**

Eco Remix Studio is a vibrant, kid-friendly DIY workshop designed to help children and parents transform everyday household items—like cardboard boxes, plastic bottles, and old fabric—into creative, practical projects.

![Project Preview](https://picsum.photos/seed/ecodiy/1200/400)

## ✨ Features

- **💡 Instant Project Finder**: Over 20+ curated DIY combinations for Cardboard, Plastic, Fabric, Glass, and Tin. Finding your next project is instant and easy!
- **📝 Interactive Planner**: A step-by-step building guide for every project. Track your progress with checkboxes and celebrate with a **Confetti Shower** when you finish!
- **⭐ AI Magic Brain Review**: Snap a photo of your finished creation and let our AI teacher (powered by Llama-3.2-Vision) give you stars and a high-five for your hard work!
- **🍭 Playful UI**: Designed specifically for kids with soft pastels, friendly fonts, and a simple 1-2-3 workflow.

## 🚀 Built With

- **HTML5 & Vanilla CSS**: A clean, modern, and ultra-responsive layout.
- **JavaScript (ESM)**: Custom interactive logic and state management.
- **WebLLM / MLC-AI**: Integrated **Llama-3.2-3B-Vision-Instruct** for on-device AI project reviews.
- **Canvas Confetti**: For those magical "I finished!" celebrations.

## 🛠️ How to Play

1. **Pick Your Stuff**: Select what material you have (like Cardboard) and what you want to make (like Storage).
2. **Find a Project**: Click "Get Project Ideas" to see instant, high-quality build guides.
3. **Follow the Steps**: Open the Project Planner to see exactly what you need and how to build it.
4. **Get a Review**: Finished your project? Snap a photo and click "Review My Work" to get star ratings from the Magic Brain!

## 🌍 Why Eco Remix?

We believe that creativity shouldn't cost the earth. By gamifying the recycling process, we help kids see "trash" as an infinite resource for fun and imagination.
